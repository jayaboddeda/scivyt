Join Us as a Researcher-Educator

We are seeking enthusiastic researchers who are passionate about teaching and knowledge sharing. As a researcher-educator at SCIVYT, you will have the chance to shape the next generation of scholars by delivering engaging courses and workshops. We value diverse perspectives and are particularly interested in individuals with expertise in various fields of study.

What We Offer:

  • Opportunities to develop and deliver innovative courses and workshops.
  • Support for continuous professional development and growth.
  • A collaborative environment that encourages interdisciplinary teaching and research.

Qualifications:

  • A Ph.D. or advanced degree in a relevant field.
  • A strong publication record and research background.
  • Excellent communication and teaching skills.
